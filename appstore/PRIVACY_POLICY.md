# Privacy Policy for YawningFace

*Last updated: [DATE]*

## Overview

YawningFace ("the App") is designed to help you stay focused by blocking distracting apps on your iPhone. We take your privacy seriously.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The App operates entirely on your device. We do not have servers, accounts, or any way to access your information.

### What stays on your device:
- Your app blocking preferences
- Your schedule settings
- Your streak data
- Screen Time permissions

### What we never see:
- Which apps you block
- When you use your phone
- Your Screen Time data
- Any personal information

## Screen Time & Family Controls

The App uses Apple's Screen Time APIs (Family Controls framework) to block apps. This is the same technology Apple uses for parental controls.

- All Screen Time data stays on your device
- Apple does not share this data with us
- We cannot see which apps you use or block

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using:
- UserDefaults (preferences)
- App Groups (for the blocking extension)

No data is transmitted over the internet.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
